MT5508 intelligent on-board video monitoring and dispatching terminal is a new generation of high-end on-board intelligent products independently developed by Qingdao Hisense Transtech Co., Ltd. It is an intelligent on-board terminal that integrates functions such as clear data collection and storage, identification and capture of vehicles occupying bus lanes, and video-based analysis of busriage congestion. The product adopts H.265/H264 video code technology, GPS/Beidou positioning technology, GPRS/3G/4G wireless communication technology, audio and video acquisition, image analysis and processing, data storage, dual CAN bus technology, RS485 bus technology, Ethernet Network interface, USB interface and other information technology. It is an ideal product for implementing public transport information construction, security monitoring and improving the service level of public transport enterprises.
